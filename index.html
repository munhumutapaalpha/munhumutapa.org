Technology For All



Who are we?

Munhumutapa is a software company centered on the development of, and investment in, social-financial technologies particularly on the continent of Africa. We are focused on improving access to financial services for all social classes across Africa. As an organization we hope to provide a blueprint for "minimum basic financial services access", to the marginalised, low-income societies of our continent through existing and emerging technologies.

Where are we based?

To best understand the problems facing African communities, we have setup in one of the most beautiful places on the continent, Victoria Falls. In this tourist city we have estabilished our headquarters from which our goal to use technology to solve some of Africa's socio-economic problems.
